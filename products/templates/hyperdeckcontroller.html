Hyperdeck Controller




Control Multiple HyperDecks with Ease


  • Centralized Control:

    Effortlessly control and monitor multiple HyperDecks from a single, convenient interface.

  • Network Flexibility:

    Manage your HyperDecks over your existing LAN network or a dedicated Ethernet connection.

  • Efficient File Management:

    Seamlessly transfer recorded files to your local storage, freeing up space on HyperDeck SD cards and SSD drives.

  • Streamlined Workflow:

    Automatically delete recordings from HyperDeck media after transfer, saving you time and storage space management.

    Works seamlessly with all models of Blackmagic Design HyperDecks.

Features

  • Master Control:

    Take command! The master button allows you to control recording, playback, and other functions on all connected HyperDecks simultaneously.

  • File Management:

    Simplify your workflow with one-click options. Download files directly to your local storage or permanently delete them from SD cards and SSD drives.

  • Independent Control:

    Need more granular control? Utilize the download-only and delete-only buttons to manage individual HyperDecks as needed.

  • Synchronized Recording:

    Ensure perfect timing across your production with adjustable delays between multiple HyperDecks.

  • Flexible Clip Management:

    Change clip cut duration anytime, adapting to your evolving project requirements.
